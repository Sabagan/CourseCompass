What We Offer

TMU Students, do you find yourself planning your university timetables but find the school only has it open per semester? Have no fear, CourseCompass is here. We offer the ability to add courses to your cart and plan out which semester of which year it belongs to. You can even set space aside for a job term by selecting its appropriate course.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I register?

You can register by clicking on the 'Register' link in the navigation bar and filling out the registration form.

How do I add courses to my cart?

To add courses to your cart, browse through the available courses and click the shopping cart icon next to the course you want to add.

Can I change my course selection?

Yes, you can change your course selection at any time by removing courses from your cart and adding new ones.
