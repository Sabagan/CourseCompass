Welcome to Course Selection Made Easy

What We Offer

TMU Students do you find yourself planning your university timetables but find the school only has it open per semester. Have no Fear, CourseCompass is here. We offer the ability to add courses to your cart and plan out which semester of which year it belongs to. You can even set space aside for job term by selecting its appropriate course

What We Offer


View all the Courses TMU has to offer and filter through by Program, Course Name, or even Course Code. Choose your search type as well, whether it be exactly the search term, starting with something or have something in the middle, we got you covered. Are you thinking of taking a course, then add it to your cart by clicking on the shopping cart next to your desired course.



All the courses you add will appear here. Do you have second thoughts about a course. If you change your mind then delete the course by clicking on the trash can next to the appropriate course. You can hover over courses but no need to select them to remove them.



This is the Course Plan, basically where you will plan out which courses from your selected courses to take during which Year and which semester. To add a course click the add button, which will bring up a selector showing all your courses, then you must choose one and click the add button. If you want to move a course to a different position, then click the remove button next to the course to remove it, and then add it where you please.



Account Management: No, you can't change your username nor password. You can change your name, date of birth, and email, and then click SAVE to save it. If you don't, it won't.
