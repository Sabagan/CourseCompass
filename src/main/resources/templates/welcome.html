About Course Compass

Course Compass is designed to help students manage their courses and academic plans effectively. With our user-friendly interface, you can easily add and organize your courses, create a detailed semester-wise plan, and track your academic progress.

  • Easy Course Management: Add, view, and delete courses with just a few clicks.
  • Comprehensive Planning: Create a detailed timetable for each semester and track your progress.
  • User-Friendly Interface: Navigate through the site with ease and efficiency.
  • Customizable Plans: Add more years to your plan if your course duration extends beyond four years.
  • Secure and Reliable: Your data is safe with us, and our system is designed for optimal performance.
  • Progress Tracking: Keep track of your completed courses and upcoming ones to ensure you meet all your academic requirements.
  • Course Recommendations: Get suggestions for courses based on your academic interests and progress.
  • Collaboration: Share your course plan with advisors or peers for better planning and advice.

Getting Started

  • Step 1: Visit the Profile page to view and manage your profile information. Ensure your details are up-to-date.
  • Step 2: Navigate to the My Courses page to add your courses. You can also view and delete courses here.
  • Step 3: Go to the Plan page to create your academic timetable. Add courses to specific semesters and customize your plan according to your needs.
  • Step 4: Regularly check and update your course plan to ensure you are on track with your academic goals. Utilize the plus buttons to add courses and keep your plan updated.
  • Step 5: Use the Recommendations page to get personalized course suggestions based on your interests and progress.
  • Step 6: Share your course plan with your advisor or peers for collaborative planning and feedback.

Features

Course Compass offers a range of features to make your academic planning seamless and efficient:

  • Course Overview: Get a comprehensive view of all your courses in one place.
  • Detailed Timetable: Plan your semesters with our detailed timetable feature. Add courses to specific semesters and track your progress.
  • Customizable Plans: Extend your plan beyond four years by adding more columns for additional years.
  • Interactive Interface: Use the plus buttons to add courses to your timetable directly from the myCourses table.
  • Data Security: Your data is secure with us, and we ensure the highest standards of privacy and security.
  • Collaboration: Share your course plan with advisors or peers to get feedback and advice on your academic journey.
  • Notifications: Receive reminders and notifications for important dates and deadlines.
  • Analytics: Track your academic progress with detailed analytics and reports.
